ONE · 一个 is a quit old project. The ver 1.0 was updated in the beginning of 2010s, designed by another Designer. Years later, in 2016, I was invited to redesigned for the ver 4.0.

This is the first time I handle the design of a whole app, and the design may seems so naive, that I was shy to face the files for a long time. Because of this, some of the files were lost, and now I try to restore this ancient stuff, and show it to you guys. Hope enjoy!


- Part 1 -

Main Pages

There are four channels in ONE, Home, a feed for all contects starts from ONE Notes, Reading, sharing the works of writers, Story of Music, sharing the experience relative to music, and Movies, sharing the reviews of Movies. In ver 4.1.0, there is also ONE Night, a radio channel to be added.


- Part 2 -

ONE Notes

I like notes, and I like write notes, whatever it is about, a trip, a book, or math notes. And no doubt, ONE Notes is my favorite thing in ONE (the original file was start whit the printmakings by Horishige and Hokusai, so at last, the best use of ONE Notes is to share Ukiyo-e, I guess). It was imerged in ONE v1.0, far earlier than I began to handle v4.0. But, unlike the colorful interface style in v1.0, I changed the style into black and white, inspire by the real books.


- Part 3 -

Text Style Sheet

Designing text style is the happiest thing during work, I can do this all day! In the designing of ONE, I designed my very first test style sheet. In iOS version, it is mainly composed by light fonts, in family PingFang, a safe fonts for symblified Chinese in apple devices -- we didn't have much money to buy fonts at that time :(

Launchings and Gods

In ONE, I designed several eggs, but only one had been coded, which is this, Launchings and Gods. Everyday in a week, you turn on this app, and you'll see a special god of this day. This is inspired by the naming of the days, for example Mecury for Monday, Apollo for Sunday, etc..
