Bilu

This is a project for sharing the experience on playing games. 5 team mates including me, among whom there were 4 programer and only one designer in the beginning, took 2 month to bring this idea to the world.

Before continuing, alow me to introduce one of my pixal work. I guess you know what this image is talking about, yeah, the Stand Alone Complex! I The reason I want to show you this, is to explain how I love games, and how I love to game (as a verb) everything I experienced including in movies or book stories.

- part 1 -

How did I work in this project?

It is show-off, but no self-proclaiming, and to be honest: teammates in Bilu called me superman. Because I entered the team as an UI/UX designer, but I also wrote PRD and most of the articles in the early period of the project, as we didn't have any write yet, and I strongly believe I am the best writer among writers they can find, at least I believed... So, allow me to start with an article about Banner Saga I wrote. (I truly hope everyone can read in Chinese such that they can read this!)


- part 2 -

Main pages

Bilu is simple, very simple. it just has a home page containing the articles we write, an album page containing some collections of articles that may have some connection -- for example, Machinarium and Lumino city may collected in a same album called the Brush of Artists, a history containing the list of our articles in the order of weeks, and, of course, article's page. What we wanted to do, is just to sharing what we feel while playing games, and that's all.


- part 3 -

Margin and padding

A minimum grid on the interface of Bilu is designed as 4pt, and, still, I tried to make the rules of margins and paddings as simple as possible, for easily coding, and for easily reading.


- part 4 -

Fonts and colors

As Bilu is such a simple project, the colors and fonts I used here are also simple. Thank to my teammates, they pick these lovely names for the colors.


- part 5 -

The articles' page

I wrote 9 articles about games for this project during the 2 months. I love writing things, and writing these articles was a great experience. Actually, most of them are not only about the game itself, but also related to the real history and some other works of art. For example, in the article about This War of Mine, I mentioned two movies about WWII, Anonyma - Eine Frau in Berlin, and Forty Four, to introduce the main idea of this game: not everyone is a soldier. I am not going to show the full articles in Behance, if you are interested, and you are confidence on your Chinese reading, you can visit Bilu.
