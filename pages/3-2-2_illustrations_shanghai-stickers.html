Shanghai stickers

Yi Sha was my design director and my personal babysitter in FangDD, the company where I worked. We worked together on a boring project which sells houses. One day, she told me she couldn't bear the uninterested work any more, and she ask me to do something interesting. It should be based on our work experience, but, also, should be nothing to do with the work itself! I know, she's asking a present rather than a work of design. So, this is what I gave her.


- part 1 -

From Shanghai, greetings

We sell houses, what ever I am actually a designer in the company, I would say, we sell houses, in Shanghai, it's nothing to be shamed about, it... just... business! So, this project should be firstly about Shanghai. Then, I should introduce some real good house in Shanghai.


- part 2 -

Some typical architecture

There are hundreds of good houses in Shanghai, some of them are very famous. Here are some typical architecture in Shanghai. Each of them is iconic to the district where it locates.


- part 3 -

Grandma's house

This is where my grand parents and their parents lived, in Luwan district. This is a very typical kind of house for living from 19 century to early 20 century. They are well architected with beautiful cyan bricks, alone the streets made of bricks too. In later 20 century, with the rising of the price of real estate in Shanghai, less and less people live there. Now, some of them are transform into coffee shop or bars or something like that.


- part 4 -

Hongkou district

This is the park near where I live now, called Lu Xun park, named after Lu Xun, the very famous writer lived there during later 19 century to early 20 century. And this sticker show the statue of Lu Xun sitting in the park. He's the one who aware the dark side in Chinese characteristic. "Every Chinese should learn Lu Xun", says my dad.

The ideal living

Well, as you have seen how good Shanghai is, still, you should know, this is a mega city with over-populous and not clean. The price of houses also make people here disappointed. But the idea of good living never changes.

The library

My wife call it a library, but actually, it is just a book shelf in our home.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​ We gather books together from all over the world. Some of them are rare, and published over 100 years.
